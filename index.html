📄 About

I work at the intersection of mathematics, physics, and computation, with a focus on understanding the structures underlying physical models and their numerical approximations. My research explores functional-analytic perspectives on stability and limits in modeling, as well as topological and geometric ideas for learning algorithms.

Based in Europe. Open to research collaborations and PhD opportunities.
